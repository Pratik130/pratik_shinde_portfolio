About

An inspiring headline about yourself.

Welcome! I’m a Data Analyst with a passion for transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. With a strong foundation in statistical analysis, data visualization, and programming, I enjoy diving into data to uncover patterns that drive strategic decision-making..

Throughout my experience, I have developed proficiency in tools like Python, SQL, and Excel, as well as in data visualization platforms such as Tableau and Power BI. I focus on clean, efficient, and data-driven solutions that add measurable value. My goal is not only to interpret data but to tell its story in a way that supports meaningful business outcomes.

Whether it’s through automating workflows, creating intuitive dashboards, or conducting in-depth analysis, I’m dedicated to using data to answer the questions that matter most. Please explore my portfolio to see examples of my work, and feel free to reach out if you’re interested in collaborating or learning more!
